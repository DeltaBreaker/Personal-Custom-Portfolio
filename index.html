About Me

My name is William. I'm a passionate software developer currently enrolled UC Berkely's Full Stack Software Development bootcamp. I have nearly 10 years of personal, self-guided experience developing software in Java for a variety of situations ranging from task automation to server applications. I've used various libraries to connect with different platforms and APIs such as Discord, Twitter, Twitch, and OpenGL. My biggest strength is my ability and motivation to learn, even on my own. Game development has always been my biggest hobby and career goal so I took that aspiration and used it as fuel to teach myself the inner workings of LWJGL 3 and its associated libraries. In just a couple of weeks I was able to develop my own 3D game engine with real time lighting and shadows which utilized common efficiency techniques such as instancing and batch sorting completely from scratch. Im always excited to add new technologies to my toolbelt and I'll jump and any and every chance I get to do so.
